Kim Kardashian squeezes into skintight latex bodysuit in hilarious KUWTK teaser The US Sun

July 2024 · 3 minute read

KIM Kardashian cursed as she struggled to squeeze into a skintight latex suit in a sneak preview of the latest episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

The famous family have got up to a range of weird and wacky things together over the last 13 years on the popular E! reality show - and in the latest season Kim and sister Kourtney, 40, decided to attend Paris Fashion Week in the most ludicrous outfits.

In the clip, the 39-year-old can be seen trying to fit her peachy bum into a mustard-colored Balmain skintight piece with the help of her makeup artists.

Realizing that the task is taking slightly longer than expected, she jokes: "S***, it's stuck to my skin."

While Kim is struggling to get into her suit, sister Kourtney is in no rush to help her sister get changed, but is in fact all ready to go with her matching brown-colored latex suit.
The 40-year-old joked: “I need to pee... I’m going to hold it.”

Kim replied: “You’re going to have to hold it until like two!”

The reality star even joked that she injured herself trying to fit into the costume.

“I literally feel like I pulled a muscle in my shoulder. This is f***ing fashion week,” she joked.

After finally fitting into the designer outfit, which shows off her hourglass figure, Kim finished off the look with a matching coat and latex gloves.

“This is like being a housewife washing dishes with gloves,” she giggled.

Despite all the fun and games during their visit to the French capital, tensions ran high between the sisters earlier on in the series.

In a dramatic trailer for the 18th season of the popular reality show, tensions reached breaking point between the sisters.
The pair were seen arguing before things turned physical.

Waving her finger in Kim’s face, Kourtney yelled: “You have nothing to say.

"Don't ever come at me like that," Kim snapped back before going to hit her.
It looks like the argument may have stemmed from the fact that Kourtney started to lose interest in appearing on the show as she felt it was unfulfilling and enjoyable for her.

She made it clear her heart wasn't in it anymore and Kim and Khloe chastised her for not putting enough effort in for the show.

Speaking about her departure from the show, Kourtney told Health Magazine: “I’m really trying to put my energy into things that are fulfilling and enjoyable.”

In the last season, Kim slammed her older sister for being so private and leaving herself and Khloe to pick up the slack.

"All of the days that Kourtney isn’t filming, Khloé and I are picking up the slack. If we’re not sharing our lives, then, what is the show?” she said.

Last November, Kourtney confirmed she was going to take a step back, after 17 series, to spend more time with her children Mason, nine, Penelope, seven and four-year-old Reign.
