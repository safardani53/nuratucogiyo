Julia Foxs Style Evolution: Photos

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Julia Fox‘s fashion is always the topic of conversation. Since stepping onto the scene in 2019, the actress has commanded attention with her bold looks.

The star made her grand debut on the fashion circuit with the premiere of Uncut Gems in 2019. As she hopped from city to city for the film’s press tour, she dressed to impress. Her style was simple and chic, ranging from sleek pantsuits and sexy dresses to risqué ensembles that had Us doing a double take.

Her biggest style moment came to pass at the Toronto leg of the premiere in September of that year. For the event, she shimmered in a mesh Paco Rabanne skirt layered over a black velour bodysuit. She teamed the look with red bottom Christian Louboutin stilettos and a matching lip.

While her wardrobe consisted of all-black looks, Fox did bring a pop of color to the CFDA Fashion Awards in November 2021. She opted for a mint green top and nude sequin skirt from Dries Van Noten, which was accessorized with Jimmy Choo shoes and jewels from Jacob & Co, Tiffany & Co. and Mateo.

A few weeks later Fox got a major makeover — literally. In January 2022 the star sparked up a romance with Kanye West. Naturally, the rapper and famed fashion fanatic made a point to woo the actress with designer duds galore.

In fact, when their second date rolled around, he surprised her with a suite full of clothing from Balenciaga, Diesel and more. “Ye had an entire hotel suite full of clothes. It was every girl’s dream come true,” Fox penned in a letter for Interview Magazine. “I don’t know how he did it or how he got all of it there in time, but I was so surprised. Like, who does things like this on a second date? Or any date!”

Though their whirlwind romance came to an end in February 2022, Fox remained consistent in the fashion department. The actress opened for LaQuan Smith that month at the label’s fall 2022 show, wearing a sexy bodycon dress that many called “the ultimate revenge dress.”

Flash-forward to New York Fashion Week in September 2022, the social media star really amped up her style.

At a Vogue party, Fox channeled her inner Maleficent in a dramatic Iris van Herpen gown that featured wing-like sleeves and a cutout bodice. She really pushed fashion boundaries at the Bloomingdale’s 150 x Harper’s Bazaar Icons bash a few days later, wearing shower curtain-esque design by Joanna Prazmo. She paired the look with a clutch and platform boots that she made from plastic, she told Harper’s Bazaar at the time.
