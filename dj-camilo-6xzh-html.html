DJ DJ Camilo was born on October 14, 1973 in United States (He's 50 years old now).

DJ and radio host known as one of the most influential Latin urban music DJs. He hosted shows including Hot 97's Take It to the Streets and The Heavy Hitter Hour. His club tours have taken him to cities including London, Paris, Amsterdam and Saint Tropez.
