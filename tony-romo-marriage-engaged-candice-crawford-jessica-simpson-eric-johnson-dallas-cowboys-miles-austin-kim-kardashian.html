EXCLUSIVE! Not Only Is Tony Romo Totally Over You, Jessica Simpson But Hes Ready To Marry His

May 2024 · 2 minute read

This has got to hurt! Your ex-boyfriend is ready to say ‘I do’ to Candice Crawford, the  girl that came just after you, Jessica!

Sorry, Jessica Simpson, but this might hurt – so we’ll try to break it to you gently. Your ex, Tony Romo, is not only ready to marry his new girlfriend, but (even worse) says he hopes you find the same happiness one day. Dagger!

“I have my eye set on one girl right now…and she’s not a Hollywood girl. She’s a girl in Dallas. Her name is Candice,” the Dallas Cowboys quarterback told HollywoodLife.com exclusively while promoting his new virtual project, Cameo Stars, in LA. When we asked the 30-year-old athlete whether a wedding ring or Super Bowl ring would come first he replied, “I don’t know. Maybe both!”

Although Jessica is finally finding love again with former pro football player Eric Johnson, it still sucks to hear that Tony is considering saying ‘I do’ to Chace Crawford’s 23-year-old sister, who also happens to be the woman he started dating immediately after their breakup. Even worse, Tony actually WANTS Jess to move on!

“I’m happy for them,” he told us, adding, “I don’t know much about it, but all I can say is, best of luck. I’m not in that world anymore, I don’t know if I could comment more on that.”

Hey Jess, the best revenge really IS to be happy. So forget Tony and concentrate on Eric – and you just might have a ring yourself one day!
