Why was Alaskan Bush People star Matt Brown not at Gabes wedding?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

MATT Brown from Alaskan Bush People missed Gabe’s wedding due to an ongoing feud.

The reality TV star was reportedly booted off the show and moved to a rehab center in California.

Here is more about the Alaskan Bush family.

Who is Matt Brown?

Matt Brown is the eldest child of Ami and Billy Brown from reality TV’s Alaskan Bush People. 

He took a break from filming the show to seek professional help for substance abuse.

Matt completed six months of combined inpatient and outpatient rehab in January 2019, and has spoken openly of his new-found sobriety on his Instagram.

Fans have not seen him on Alaskan Bush People since season 8, as he was dealing with his substance abuse issues while the show filmed its ninth and tenth seasons.

However, he has started his own YouTube Channel, which only features a handful of videos so far.

Why was he not at Gabe’s wedding? 

Matt was not at his brother Gabe’s wedding due to tension between him and his father Billy.

Gabe and his wife Raquell got married in June of 2019 in an outdoor ceremony alongside friends and family. 

Most of the family were spotted in the wedding photos except for Matt, who missed the wedding after reportedly being upset he was fired from the reality series.

What is the tension in the family?

Rumors have swirled that the Brown family have not been getting along for quite some time now.

While Matt decided to leave the family home to deal with his health, many of his siblings parted ways with Billy and Ami too.

Noah Brown and his wife, Rhain, recently moved to Colorado with their new son Elijah. 

Bam Bam reportedly lives with his girlfriend, producer Allison Kagan, and Gabe has also reportedly moved out since his wedding.

“Bam Bam and Allison want nothing to do with his family," a source told Radar Online.

“The only reason that Bam Bam even was on the show is because he has to in order for them to keep the contracts. 

“He does not like his mother and father because of the way that they treat Allison."

Gabe and Raquell recently welcomed a baby into their family and want to keep the child’s life as private as possible.
