Who is Justin Jefferson?

Justin Jefferson was born on June 16, 1999, to John Jefferson and Elaine Jefferson in St. Rose, Louisiana. He is a brother of two, Jordan and Rickey Jefferson.

The NFL player was born into a family of athletes. His father played basketball at the Division 11 level. Justin and his brothers are NFL players. 

Career

Justin has played football since his days at Destrehan High School. After high school, he chose Louisiana State University as his college.

There, he participated in collegiate football for three years until the Vikings selected him in the first round of the 2020 NFL Draft.

Justin agreed to a four-year, $13.12 million rookie contract with the Vikings, including a $7.1 million signing bonus. He made his NFL debut against the Green Bay Packers and recorded his first two receptions in 26 years.

Justin faced numerous worthy opponents and was selected for the 2021 Pro Bowl and the 2020 season’s Rookie of the Year by the sporting news.

In the 2021 season, Justin won NGC Offensive Player of the Week and, eventually, NFC Offensive Player of the Month honors for his 464 yards and three touchdowns in four games.

Justin was selected to the AP All-Pro second team for the second year in a row, losing out on a berth on the first team by one vote.

As of Week 16, 2022, the young player has a stat of 319 receptions, 4,772 receiving yards, and 25 receiving touchdowns.

Justin is giving his best to the game and is getting a lot of praise for it.
