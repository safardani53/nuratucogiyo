Dunkin customers have just two days left to take advantage of limited menu offer see the spec

March 2024 · 2 minute read

DUNKIN' fans only have a couple of days left to take advantage of a special offer – but not every menu item will qualify for the deal.

Throughout the entire month of May, the fast-food chain has been offering what it calls "free coffee Mondays."

Customers who make any purchase qualify for a free medium coffee.

But since the month is waning down, only two Mondays are left to take advantage of the deal.

So you'll need to either act today or Monday, May 29.

But keep in mind, not everyone can take advantage of the deal on Mondays.
