Netflix actress shows off stunning 100k Maserati after starring in M&amp;S and Baileys Christmas adver

August 2024 · 2 minute read

BRIT actress Hannah Waddingham has been spotted in a luxury supercar worth £100k - as she prepares to host a glitzy award show.

The rising star, who's riding high after co-hosting Eurovision back in May, is set to host the Earthshot Prize Awards alongside Prince William in Singapore tonight.

The 49-year-old, who's currently the face of Christmas adverts for both M&S and Baileys, was seen motoring around south-west London in her new whip, which appears to be a Maserati Levante Trofeo.

The Italian motor retails for up to £143k and offers an 'exclusive, sporty take on the luxury SUV'.

The Ferrari-engined beauty boasts 72bhp and can go 0-62mph in around four seconds, with a stunning top speed of 190mph.

According to the Daily Mail, the Maserati purchase comes as Hannah's company HK Waddingham Ltd is shown to have 'current assets' of nearly £1.5million.
