Who is Andi Peters and is he dating anyone? The Sun

February 2024 · 3 minute read

ANDI Peters has become a TV staple since starting his career on CBBC.

He now is often seen on the competition segments on Good Morning Britain - here's everything to know about him.

Who is Andi Peters?

Andi Peters was born on July 29, 1970, in London before going to school in Battersea in South West London.

His full name is Andi Eleazu Peters and he was born to Nigerian parents.

His early breaks in the showbiz world came on children's TV with shows on CBBC - he also presented popular kids' show Live & Kicking for three years.

Andi's exit from the show in 1996 was voted one of the biggest tear-jerking moments in TV history by Channel 4.

The presenter has a net worth of around £3.1 million, built up from his many years in television.

Has Andi Peters been married?

Andi tends to keep his personal life very private.

He has never spoken about being married, and it has never been suggested that has been married in the past.
In 2016, he tweeted a picture of a sign which read: "I'm single because I'm a superhero."
Alongside this he wrote the caption: "Now that's a fact I like!" which has led some to think he had confirmed his relationship status.

What TV shows has Andi Peters been in?

Andi took part in the first series of Dancing On Ice on ITV before going on to present the fan show Dancing On Ice Extra with fellow former contestant Andrea McLean.

Since then he has hosted the Dancing On Ice tour - where popular contestants take their ice skating skills on the road around Britain - ever since 2007.

In 2008, Andi appeared in the third series of Celebrity MasterChef on the BBC.

He reached the final - finishing as runner-up behind Atomic Kitten singer Liz McClarnon.

Andi also narrated ITV2's The Big Reunion and is currently in charge of the competition segments of Good Morning Britain, working alongside Piers Morgan and Susanna Reid.

Occasionally he will present This Morning when regular presenters are taking time off.

He is also a radio regular who used to work alongside Chris Moyles on his BBC Radio 1 show.

In 2013 he started presenting his own show on Smooth Radio on

a Sunday afternoon.

Is Andi Peters dating anyone?

As of 2022, Andi is not in a relationship.

When he presented Loose Women in a shake up alongside Jordan Banjo, Ore Oduba and The Chase's "The Dark Destroyer" Shaun Wallace, he confirmed he was single.

They were talking about going on a lads holiday when Andi said: "See, I'm single, so I just go by myself."

The audience sighed as Andi replied: "Please don't feel sorry for me! It's through choice. I'm really stingy.

"I only have to buy a drink for myself and nobody else.

"I quite enjoy just going by myself, just being completely in control, I don't have to ask anyone's permission."
