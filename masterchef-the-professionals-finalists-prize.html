MASTERCHEF: The Professionals is heading into its grand finale. Tension builds as s the hopeful finalists prepare to take home that coveted trophy that has seen 13 other chefs do the same

What does the winner of MasterChef: The Professionals get?

The winner of MasterChef: The Professionals is awarded a very prestigious title and a snazzy trophy. Plus they get to boast about the fact they were most impressive in the eyes of the esteemed judges. But there is no cash prize on MasterChef: The Professionals, just the honour of winning the show.

Where are the past winners now?

Alex Webb 

Alex was crowned the winner of MasterChef: Professionals 2020.

The ambitious chef from Essex impressed the judges throughout his time in the competition with his elegant dishes bringing a sense of fun to the occasion.

Alex said: "It is the best feeling I have ever had in my life. To walk away with the trophy is incredible. I am so proud."

Stu Deeley

Due to the pandemic affecting the hospitality industry, Stu's plans to open his own restaurant were put on hold until September 2021, when he finally opened Smoke in the grounds of Hampton Manor, Solihull.

The Nottingham-based chef has stayed put in his hometown and in September 2021 announced that he would be taking the reigns of Canal Turn, a brand new restaurant in The Island Quarter.

Canal Turn is set to open in early 2022, but an exact date hasn't yet been confirmed.

Craig Johnston

Craig Johnston became the show's youngest ever winner in 2017.

Craig now works for judge Marcus Wareing in his restaurant, Marcus in London's Berkeley hotel.

He began as a sous-chef, but was promoted to head chef at the age of just 26 in November 2021.

Gary Maclean

Gary won the show in 2016.

He would go on to become Scotland's first ever National Chef.

Gary is now a senior chef lecturer at the City of Glasgow College, where he used to be a student.

Mark Stinchcombe

After winning the competition in 2015 he stayed working at Eckington Manor in Worcestershire for three years, working alongside his wife Sue, who was the co-head chef.

They now live in Gloucestershire with their kids and Mark works for The Severn & Wye Smokery.

Mark can be found on Instagram at @markstinchcombe, where he regularly posts snaps of his cooking and life with his family.

Jamie Scott

Since his win in 2014, Jamie has run a number of pop-up restaurants.

He's now Head Chef at The Newport, which he opened in 2016 with his wife Kelly.

The restaurant has since earned an entry in the Michelin Guide.

Steven Edwards

Steven has his own restaurant, The Etch in Hove, Brighton, as of 2017.

He won the show in 2013 at the age of just 26.

In 2020, he expanded his empire and opened a new restaurant - Steven Edwards @ The Bingham Riverhouse - in Richmond, London.

Anton Piotrowski and Keri Moss

In 2012, two chefs were crowned the winner for the first time in the show's history.

Keri works as a food designer and menu developer for corporate clients and events.

In October 2021, she participated in The Great Nordic Feast - working with Team Sweden.

Ash Mair

After winning in 2011, he wrote a book entitled My Basque Cuisine. At the time, Ash was working as a freelance Chef at a number of different places.

Since winning the show, Ash has worked as a development chef - helping to build businesses and restaurants.

Claire Lara

Claire won in 2010, when she was working as a catering teacher.

She is now a development chef with her husband at Liverpool's Hilton Hotel restaurant, Koukash.

Steve Groves

Steve won the second series of the show in 2009.

He became head chef at Roux at Parliament Square and in 2020 was awarded National Chef of the Year.

Derek Johnstone

Derek was the first chef to be crowned winner of MasterChef: The Professionals all the way back in 2008.

He is currently Borthwick Castle's head chef.

In 2018, he was recognised for his efforts with the Scottish Event Chef of The Year award.

When is the MasterChef: The Professionals 2021 final?

The grand final will be available on December 16, 2021, at 9pm on BBC One.

All previous episodes are available on the iPlayer.

MasterChef: The Professionals kicked off on November 8, 2021.
