  • All about Jingle Ball

    The KIIS-FM Jingle Ball (sometimes known simply as Jingle Ball) is an annual concert held in early-to-mid December by the Los Angeles radio station KIIS-FM.

    The concert series has been held at several locations around southern California since 2000, including the Shrine Auditorium and Staples Center in Los Angeles, as well as the Honda Center in Anaheim.

    Since 2011, iHeartMedia sister stations such as WHYI in Miami, WXKS in Boston, and WIOQ in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, have staged their own Jingle Balls as part of the iHeartRadio Jingle Ball Tour series.
