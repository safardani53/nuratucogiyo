Lisa Vanderpump under fire after a 'BUG was discovered inside Vanderpump Ros wine bottle'

July 2024 · 3 minute read

LISA Vanderpump has faced backlash after a BUG was found in her Vanderpump Rosé wine bottle.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum has been shocking her viewers with wild behavior on her new show Overserved.

In a series of photos obtained by PageSix, several earwigs were found in a batch of Lisa's 2018 Vanderpump Rosé.

The bottles were delivered to a Las Vegas liquor store, where the bugs were quickly discovered lying inside the bottom of the bottles.

“I recommend everyone thoroughly inspect any bottle of Vanderpump Rosé before purchasing and consuming them,” a source urged.

The insider believed that the bottling facility was to blame for the crisis, as the insects very doubtfully entered the bottles in Provence, France where the grapes are grown.

The distributer for the rosé, Palm Bay International, launched a statement on Wednesday claiming they have not had any such complaints in the past.

“This is the first we are learning of it. We stand behind the quality and integrity of the wines in our portfolio and require rigorous standards at the production and bottling facilities that we work with," a company representative assured.

“We’d welcome the chance to connect directly with the individual who encountered this issue, as in five years of selling this product we have never received any retailer or customer complaints of this nature. Thank you!”

Lisa Vanderpump, 60, has yet to make comment on the terrifying situation.

In March, the Bravo star encountered more hospitality complications when her Los Angeles restaurant Pump was suspended from opening after she failed to pay a $250 tax-related fine.

“So important, worthy of a headline…said no one ever. Geez,” she tweeted sarcastically, adding: “10,000 [sic] restaurants have closed for good."

Lisa has been engaging in wild behavior on her show Overserved, where she hosts and entertains famous dinner guests.

During last week's episode the restauranteur invited Jackass star Steve-O to the table, who she let draw a temporary tattoo of a penis on her chest during the evening.

Steve-O and comedian Margaret Cho were made to race on floats across Lisa's pool in an Olympic style game.

The British TV personality won the competition, as Lisa then allowed him to ink her chest.

"I think I'd rather have it on my chest than my face," the hostess confessed, before asking: "How did I get into this mess?"

Later in the night Steve-O was forced to snort chili powder, while Lisa looked on happily.

"I feel so much better about the d*** on my chest," she laughed.

Lisa recently opened up about her emotional and mental state while on Real Housewives, as she admitted that "all the fighting" put her in a "dark hole."

“I found it very difficult, a dark hole to walk out of," she told HollywoodLife.
