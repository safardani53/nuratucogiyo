Taylor Swifts You All Over Me: What are the lyrics?

July 2024 · 3 minute read

MEGA music star Taylor Swift announced on Instagram that she was releasing a special song “from the vault” with her re-release of Fearless.

The musician will be releasing the new song at midnight ET on March 25.

What are the lyrics to Taylor Swift’s You All Over Me?

Taylor Swift will be releasing a secret song, You All Over Me, as part of her re-release of her studio album, Fearless.

Though the song comes out at midnight, lyrics have already been posted to the Genius website so fans can prepare for the release.

The song starts off saying: “Once the last drop of rain has dried off the pavement, Shouldn't I find a stain, but I never do.

"The way the tires turn stones on old county roads, you leave it muddy underneath, reminds me of you.

"You find graffiti on the walls of old bathroom stalls, you know, you can scratch it right off, it's how we used to be.

"Like the dollar in your pocket, that's been spent and traded in, you can't change where it's been, reminds me of me.

The chorus says: “I lived, and I learned, had you, got burned, held out, held on.

God knows, too long, you wasted my time, lost tears, swore that I'd get out of here, but no amount of freedom gets you clean. I still got you all over me,”

The song was produced by Aaron Dessner from the band The National and was co-written with Scooter Carusoe.

Swift also announced that there will be background vocals sung by country star Marren Morris.

When is Taylor Swift releasing Fearless?

A date for the re-release of Fearless (Taylor’s version) has not yet been confirmed.

Another added: “YOU DID NOT JUST WRITE THAT WHOLE THING AND ADD A SECRET MESSAGE GOD YOURE TAKING ME BACK.”

A user chimed in: “APRIL NINTH WHAT?????” with one replying: “Probably the release of the whole album??”

Did Taylor win Album of the Year at the Grammys?

Swift took home the Album of the Year award at the 2021 Grammys for her eighth album, Folklore.

She is the first woman to win the award three times in Grammy history.

Swift won the award for her albums Fearless, in 2010, and 1989, in 2016.

Frank Sinatra, Paul Simon and Stevie Wonder are the only other artists to win the Album of the Year category three times.

During her acceptance speech, Taylor gave her longtime boyfriend Joe Alwyn a sweet, little shout-out.
