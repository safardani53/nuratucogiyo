Applebees brings back fan favorite deal with a new meat option but theres a catch

March 2024 · 2 minute read

RESTAURANT goers can grab another deal as Applebees has launched its fan-favorite pairing, with a twist.

The “2 for $25” deal is a popular offering that includes two full-size entrées, a choice of one appetizer, or two side salads.

But for a limited time only, Applebee's fans now have the option of a six-ounce top sirloin.

“We know how much our guests love our ongoing 2 for $25 menu, and now with a six-ounce top sirloin available for a limited time, guests can enjoy the best of the best – for the same great price,” Joel Yashinsky, chief marketing officer, Applebee’s said. 

Customers can receive the deal for dine-in, to-go, and delivery.

The entrée items to choose from also include the Fiesta Lime Chicken and the Classic Cheeseburger, and more.

For appetizers, guests can pick from items like Boneless Wings, Mozzarella Sticks, or Spinach & Artichoke Dip.

If customers would rather have a salad, guests can swap out the appetizer for two side salads instead.

Just note that the offer is only for a limited time and prices and food selections could vary.

The company has also added new cocktails just in time for the summer.

Guests can try $6 sips with its tropical-inspired Mucho Cocktails menu.

Each is made with premium spirits and served in its signature Mucho glass.

Plus, at participating locations, customers can enjoy their sips of paradise at home and order them to-go.

Drinks include Passion on the Beach with Tito’s Handmade Vodka, peach schnapps, orange juice and Ocean Spray Cranberry.

For the classic cocktail lovers, Applebee's suggests the Mucho Mai Tai featuring Captain Morgan Rum blended with pineapple, lime, and orange juices all topped with lemon-lime soda and a pineapple gummi.

RETURNING EATS

In addition to your favorite 2 for $25 deal, other major chains are bringing fan favorites back.

Back by popular demand, Krispy Kreme has added four fan favorites that were the top-selling, limited-edition flavors over the past five years.

Customers can score the banana pudding doughnut, chocolate Kreme pie doughnut, key lime pie doughnut, and strawberries and Kreme doughnut.

If no stores are located near you, select grocery stores will be selling assortment boxes that are delivered fresh daily.

However, these flavors aren't back for good and will only be available while supplies last.
