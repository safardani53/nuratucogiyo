'Marilyn Manson called me a dirty Mexican wh**e &amp; made me have sex with his pals while he watched,'

April 2024 · 8 minute read

AN EX-TEEN lover of Marilyn Manson has claimed he called her "a dirty Mexican wh**e" and made her have sex with his friends while he watched during their "emotionally abusive" relationship, The Sun can reveal.

Actress Bianca Allaine alleged the shock rocker forcefully kissed her in 1995, when she was just 16, before the pair began hooking up three years later, during which he would regularly "humiliate " her.

She also claimed she saw Manson groping, kissing and fondling a number of her underaged friends.

Bianca branded him "Jekyll and Hyde" and claimed the pair would drink and snort cocaine during the wild three-month relationship, and he would often see "how much he could get away with" when it came to belittling her.

Her claims come after a string of allegations, ranging from psychological abuse to domestic violence, made by a number of women including the singer's ex-fiancee Evan Rachel Wood.

Speaking exclusively to The Sun, Bianca said: "It was a very confusing relationship, and looking back I feel it was emotionally abusive.

"I was in love with him and I thought it was mutual. I just wanted a regular relationship with him.

"Now that I've actually had love in my life, I know that wasn't love. I don't know what I was thinking.

"He would be very sweet one minute, and then he'd degrade me in front of his friends.

"When he was with his entourage or a group of guys, he would say things to me, that I was fat, I was 5 foot 10 and 120 pounds at the time.

"The worst was he once told me I looked like a dirty Mexican wh**e because I have olive skin.

"He would purposely say things like that to make me feel bad about myself.

"It was just funny to him, he liked to see how much he could get away with.

"He would say things like, 'We gotta see your biscuits, Bianca', talking about my breasts.

"And he would get [a friend] to pull down my top and fondle them, he would do things like that all the time."

The 41-year-old brunette said she was a huge fan of Manson and first met him after his performance on September 15, 1995 at Deep Ellum Live in Dallas, Texas during his Smells Like Children tour.

A 16-year-old virgin at the time, she claimed she waited to meet the star at his tour bus with a group of younger friends and was stunned when he acted "inappropriately," sitting them on his lap, groping them and French kissing her.

"I was kind of pulling away and he actually put his hand behind my head and pulled me back in and began to French kiss me for a few seconds," she said.

"I just got off the bus and walked off. I didn't know what to think, I was like, is this good? Is this bad? What's happening? I'm scared.

"I'd barely had boyfriends before, and here's this guy that I really look up to and he's doing that."

Bianca claimed he whispered in one girl's ear that he wanted to "bite" her, and she feels now he was "grooming" them as she returned with friends to his future concerts.

"He was giving alcohol and drugs to underage girls and touching them, all the signs were there," she said. "This man is a predator and he is so smug."

She says she moved to Los Angeles and was with Manson almost every day for a three month period beginning in March 1999 while he was on tour.

They had a rollercoaster relationship where she'd do anything she could to please him, admitting she was "young and naive."

"He was never violent towards me, but I believe the women who have come forward and feel I've dodged a bullet in a lot of ways," she said.

The Sun has reached out to Marilyn's rep for comment, but did not receive a response.

"He did hit his assistant at the time, he would beat him up, he would give him $100 every time if he'd gotten too high.

"We were often never alone on the tour bus, and his fetish back then was to watch people have sex with me or do things to me.

"A lot of times I didn't want to have sex with these guys, but he was like 'Please, please, I really need it, I need to see it.'

"He would masturbate and if he didn't finish he'd want to have sex with me afterwards, he would bite me so hard."

Tearful Bianca said she feels like Manson used her.

"I was like a little puppet that he would play with, I feel he used me, 100 percent, he didn't care about me," she said.

"It was like, 'How low can I get someone to go to please me?' That's his M.O, he wants to degrade you. And he gets pleasure out of that. He's a sadist.

"I feel for the other girls who are coming forward, there are still others I've spoken to who are afraid.

"I just want people to know these habits started at least in 1995, before he was really famous."

Evan Rachel Wood showed her support for Bianca by sharing an initial post on her Instagram account about her time with the musician in the late 90s.

Wood, along with four other women, came forward last Monday with their own claims of psychological and physical abuse by the rocker, 52, who is yet to step out in public amid the scandal.

Other women have since gone public with similar stories.

Actress Evan claimed her ex-fiance "horrifically abused her for years" and she now wants to "expose" this "dangerous man."

She claimed on Instagram: "The name of my abuser is Brian Warner, also known to the world as Marilyn Manson.

"He started grooming me when I was a teenager and horrifically abused me for years. I was brainwashed and manipulated into submission. I am done living in fear of retaliation, slander, or blackmail. 

"I am here to expose this dangerous man and call out the many industries that have enabled him, before he ruins any more lives.

"I stand with the many victims who will no longer be silent."

It is not yet known whether the women will file a class action lawsuit against the Tainted Love singer, or if he will face a police probe.

However, Evan shared a letter Senator Susan Rubio sent to FBI Director Christopher Wray and Acting US Attorney General Monty Wilkinson.

"The alleged victims have named Marilyn Manson, also known as Brian Hugh Warner, as the perpetrator," Rubio wrote in the letter dated January 21.

"I ask that the US Department of Justice meet with the alleged victims immediately and investigate these accusations."

Marilyn posted a statement on his Instagram last Tuesday, vehemently denying the allegations. 

He wrote: “Obviously, my art and my life have long been magnets for controversy, but these recent claims about me are horrible distortions of reality. 

“My intimate relationships have always been entire consensual with like-minded partners. 

"Regardless of how – and why – others are now choosing to misrepresent the past, that is the truth."

Bianca, who supported Manson at more than 25 shows, said her relationship with him ended after the star and his team suddenly cut her off, and she was forced to face the fact she didn't mean anything to him.

"All of them ghosted me, even the assistant and it made me feel horrible, so bad that I just left LA, I moved back to Dallas," she told The Sun.

"It just made my drug problem worse. And I was starting to have an eating disorder and it just escalated. I hated myself.

"I was like, what did I do wrong? I even looked through my journals and I just kept saying, what did I do?"

Bianca, who has been clean for years and is living and working as an actress in Maryland, says it's time for women allegedly abused by Manson to "take their power back" and slammed his statement.

"He's a complete liar. It's just total BS because I witnessed things like this," Bianca said.

"He likes breaking people and he kind of gets off on all of it.

"And I think the reason he went off so much on Evan is because she probably finally just put her foot down and said no more.

"It probably enraged him because he couldn't control her anymore."
