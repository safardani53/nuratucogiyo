Jennifer Hale is a Canadian-American actress. She is best known through video game franchises such as Baldur's Gate, Mass Effect, Metal Gear Solid, BioShock Infinite, Metroid Prime, Overwatch and Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. In 2013, she was recognized by the Guinness World Records as the highest-paid video-game voice actress. Hale has appeared in cartoons like Jonny Quest's Real Adventures, The Powerpuff Girls, Codename: Kids Next Door, The Grim Adventures of Billy & Mandy, Brandy & Mr. Whiskers, Totally Spies !, Avatar: The Last Airbender and its sequel The Legend of the Korra, Star Wars: Clone Wars and Where's Carmen Sandiego? She also voiced Cinderella and Princess Aurora in various Disney Princess media from 2000 and 2010
