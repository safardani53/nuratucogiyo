March 2024 · 3 minute read

A KARDASHIANS sneak peek has has given fans a look at Kris Jenner's massive backyard and stunning pool inside her new $20million mansion.

Kris, 66, recently moved into LA's Hidden Hills where she is neighbors with daughter Khloe, 37.

The promo for next week's episode, posted to the Hulu original's Instagram account, shows the tranquil pool in all its beauty.

It serves as a focal point on the expansive lawn, interrupting the long stretch of green with the shimmering blue of the water.

The crystal clear depths of the pool are framed by a white marble walkway while four massive stone planters are positioned at each corner.

No diving boards or slides, this swimming hole is meant for relaxing only.

Last week's Kardashians featured other areas of the momager's new build.

During the episode, Kris has a dinner party celebrating her birthday at the 20M mansion.

Before the meal begins, she treats several of her celebrity guests to a tour of her newly completed home.

On one such tour, Kourtney Kardashian, 43, and then-fiancé, now husband, Travis Barker, 46, are taken to see the gym.

The Blink-182 drummer is immediately impressed saying, "I love your gym" as he takes in the mirrored room.

The gym is large enough to accommodate an entire workout class.

Black workout equipment is positioned purposefully around the room with consideration given to how a gym should flow.

A large flatscreen TV is mounted facing a line of cardio machines and a beverage refrigerator is tucked away neatly inside one of the walls.

Kourtney states that she "loves that [the gym] is really infused with our personality.

'LET'S ALL GO TO THE MOVIES'

Another standout of the episode was Kris's stunning state-of-the-art home movie theater.

The screening room was complete with a massive screen, two rows of seating, dimmable light sconces lining the walls, and finished off with comfy pillows and $1.7k Hermes blankets for cuddling.

Viewers were also able to catch a glimpse of the Kardashian momager's over-the-top kitchen, as her daughter Khloe Kardashian, 37, gushed over her crystal glassware.

On the evening of the matriarch's birthday, the family, and friends including Ellen DeGeneres and Portia DeRossi, and Faye Resnick, arrived at the mansion for her special dinner.

Her huge white kitchen featured massive matte black fridges, and a see-through one filled to the brim with fresh green vegetables.
