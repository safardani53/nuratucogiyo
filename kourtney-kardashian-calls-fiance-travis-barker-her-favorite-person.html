KOURTNEY Kardashian has called her fiancé Travis Barker her "favorite person" after the drummer shared a close-up of the tattoo she inked for him.

The Blink 182 star, who has over 100 tattoos on his body, took to Instagram Story last night to show off his "favorite tattoo" that Kourtney, 42, gave him in May.

The post featured close-up of the words "I love you" written in Kourtney's handwriting on his forearm and also a link to a YouTube video of her showing off her tattoo artist skills.

Captioning the shot, the musician, 46, penned: "My favorite tattoo from my favorite tattoo artist".

Feeling the love, the KUWTK star then reposted Travis' photo to her own Instagram Story and wrote across it: "only for my favorite person".

This isn't Travis' only tattoo tribute to Kourtney. In October, he showed off a new tattoo on his bicep that featured Kourtney's lips.

The rocker revealed the new art as he covered up an older tattoo dedicated to ex-wife Shanna Moakler.

In an Instagram snap shared by tattoo artist Scott Campbell, Travis' arm could be seen with a new scorpion design with Kourtney's lips at the end of the tail.
