Who is Jake Paul's girlfriend Julia Rose?

April 2024 · 2 minute read

CONTROVERSIAL YouTube star Jake Paul is well known around the world, thanks to his highly publicised antics.

One aspect of his life that constantly hits the headlines, is his on/off relationship with Julia Rose.

Who is Julia Rose?

Born in New Orleans on December 30, 1993, Julia Rose is an Instagram model and YouTube star.

She is the girlfriend of controversial YouTuber Jake Paul.

Julia is best known as the World Series Flasher.

Her social media is filled with lots of sexy selfies - you can follow her @its_juliarose.

When did Julia Rose start dating Jake Paul?

The pair went Instagram official with their romance in February 2020.

The couple met on the set of his music video for These Days.

At the time, Jake said that he’d be keeping their relationship private: “I have been talking to a girl a little bit,” he claimed during an appearance on The Night Shift podcast. 

Did Julia Rose split up with Jake Paul?

The couple had a rocky 2020 - the two allegedly split in the spring, but in May, Jake was reported as saying they were together again.

However, in 2021 they split again.

Speaking to ET Online, at the time Jake said: "I was getting mad at her and then I was being mean, and she was being mean, and then it just was a lot. So she was like, 'I need to step back from this'."

He then described how he "chased" after her to convince her to get back together - he even rented a $2,000 billboard to get her attention.

In March 2022, Jake and Julia split up again.

Sources told PageSix: "Paul has been out and about in Miami telling pals that he and Rose are indeed done" and that "he’s devastated".

They added: "He would like to get back together with her and maybe they will, but they both need to slow down their lives."

As of 2023 they are back together again.

Why is Julia Rose known as the 'World Series Flasher'?

In 2019 Julia, along with her friend Lauren Summer, both lifted their shirts to flash Houston Astros pitcher Gerrit Cole.

It was during Game 5 of the MLB World Series.
