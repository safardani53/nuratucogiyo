Justin Bieber Totally Cheated on Selena Gomez!

April 2024 · 3 minute read

It’s only Monday and we have been hit with a week’s worth of drama, all thanks to Scooter Braun’s new controversial business deal.

We’re really trying to keep up with the mayhem, but it seems every time we turn around someone else is getting dragged into the fire. 

It all started this weekend when celebrity manager Scooter Braun acquired Taylor Swift’s music catalog through a $300 million deal with Big Machine Records. 

Swift, who has six albums under the label, called the recent move her "worst case scenario" in an intense (yet fair) Tumblr post which she created after finding out the news — at the same time as the rest of us! 

Naturally, the singer didn’t hold back and continued ranting to her followers, calling out not only Braun for bullying her in the past, but Kim Kardashian, Justin Bieber, and Kanye West. 

It was bold. 

But not as bold as when Bieber decided to reply to Swift’s lengthy rant on his own Instagram page … creating a very public and very entertaining awkward situation. 

Tay hasn’t replied to Justin directly, but she is still remaining active in all the drama as she continues to like fans’ comments about him via her Tumblr page. 

And fans lost it after her most recent click, which totally insinuated that Bieber cheated on his ex and Swift’s bestie Selena Gomez. 

Taylor has not confirmed these cheating rumors directly, but fans think that liking the comment was an indirect confirmation. 

The post read: 

"’We haven’t gotten to communicate our difference.’ You cheated on her best friend and then publicly sided with the man who made revenge porn against her, was she supposed to invite you over for tea???"

"F–k outta here," the post concluded. 

It still remains liked on her page after Twitter quickly picked up on Swift’s subtle confirmation that there was an issue between Gomez and Bieber. 

The two dated on-again, off-again for years before they finally called it quits in March of 2018. 

Bieber is now married to Hailey Baldwin, who also took a chance to stick up for her man on his recent Instagram post, writing "Gentleman."

But Cara Delevingne, who is a part of Taylor’s fierce girl squad, caught glimpse of her comment and wasn’t having it. 

"Gentleman?" she said tagging both Hailey and Justin. "You must be bored."

She then went on to address the situation stating that Justin’s message was far from an apology and that they should be lifting women up and not tearing them down. 

"As a married man, you should be lifting women up instead of tearing them down because you are threatened."

"I am not sure you actually understand what an apology is," she continued. 

"As you said, you haven’t spoken to her in years which means you definitely don’t understand the situation. I do."

"Take a step back and learn from this. We should all be on the same team," she concluded.

(mic drop.) 

Cara hasn’t been the only one to publicly side with Taylor though.

Many other celebrities including Halsey and Victoria Secret model Martha Hunt are standing up for the "ME!" singer. 

As well as all of her Swifties, of course. 

Selena has yet to comment on her friend’s situation, as well as the cheating rumors which Swift brought about by just one simple click. 

Will Bieber take to his social media accounts yet again? This time to address his possible past of infidelity?

We really don’t know what will happen or who will speak out next on Scooter v. Swift, but stay tuned because this tea is hot enough to last us probably up until Tay’s next diss track.
