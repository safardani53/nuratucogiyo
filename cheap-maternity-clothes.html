9 Cheapest and Best Value Maternity Clothes 2020 | The Sun UK

May 2024 · 6 minute read

MATERNITY clothes are essential to dress a growing bump but that doesn't mean you need to spend a fortune on your pregnancy wardrobe.

There are plenty of cheap maternity clothes options out there from a range of your favourite high street brands, like H&M, Zara and New Look. We’ve rounded up what we consider to be the best cheap maternity clothes from the brands you need to know about.

A good pair of maternity jeans, some maternity trousers, a maternity bra and a couple of maternity dresses are the basics to get you through the nine-month pregnancy stretch in style.

However, you may also want to invest in a couple of nursing tops, some maternity activewear or a bathing suit, and, if you have any events – there’s nothing like being eight-months pregnant during summer wedding season, believe us – you may want something fun to celebrate an occasion.

What shops do maternity wear?

Almost every high-street store now sells some form of maternity wear.
There are the obvious shops, such as Mothercare and Mamas & Papas.
However, New Look, H&M, Marks & Spencer and Zara, in particular, also sell maternity wear at an affordable price.
Even supermarkets now offer a range of maternity clothes in their larger stores.
That’s not to mention online brands such as Asos and Boohoo.

Do you buy maternity clothes in your normal size?

Yes. You buy maternity clothes in your normal size wherever possible.
For example, if you’re normally a medium, you’ll still need to buy a medium from a store’s maternity range.
Similarly, if a dress in a maternity range is a size 14, it’s not actually a regular size 14.
The shops label it as such to help you choose and many ranges have stretchy panels or extra fabric to accommodate your growing bump.
Of course, some bumps are larger than others, regardless of your starting size, but this is a good guide.
Don’t be tempted to get larger sizes in pregnancy clothes, though, as they won’t fit as well in other places and will make you look larger than you are.

When should I buy maternity clothes?

You should buy maternity clothes as soon as your normal wardrobe is starting to pinch or feel uncomfortable.
You’ll likely find you need to buy a pair of maternity jeans pretty soon, but some of your dresses might last a little longer.
