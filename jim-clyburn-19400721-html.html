James Enos Clyburn is an American politician and a Democrat from the US House of Representatives from South Carolina. He has held the position of a majority of the whips in parliament since 2019. He is a dual majority, served from 2007 to 2011 and was deputy leader of the minority faction from 2011 to 2019. Currently 15th year of Congress, Clyburn has served as the United States representative for South Carolina's Sixth Electoral District since 1993. His Congressional District includes most of the majority constituencies. blacks in and around Columbia and Charleston, as well as nearly all of South Carolina's black belt. Clyburn is the current chairman of the South Carolina congressional delegation. After John Spratt left in 2011, in addition to Joe Cunningham's sole term, Clyburn was the only Democrat in the South Carolina congressional delegation.
