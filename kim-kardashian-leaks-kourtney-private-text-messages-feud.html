KIM Kardashian has exposed her sister Kourtney's private text messages amid their nasty feud in a new video.

The siblings have been embroiled in an ongoing fight that played out on the newest season of their reality show, The Kardashians.

During the series' first episode that dropped on Thursday, a clip showed Kim, 42, reading the harsh words Kourtney, 44, sent her in front of the cameras.

While sitting on the floor of her closet in a tight black ensemble and her formerly blonde locks flowing down, she said: "She texted me and goes: You have no loyalty and no boundaries. It's like a f***ing free for all with you."

No further context was given as the scene will air on a future episode, although the texts were presumed to be from Kourtney.

However, another teaser showed Kim snapping at Kourtney for "ditching everyone" to be with her husband, Travis Barker, 47.

Looking exhausted, Kourtney replied: "I don't want to be a part of this where the paycheck is more important than the people's feelings."

The sisters' feud appears to be a large storyline in the show's third season, also involving the third Kardashian sister, Khloe, 38.

SISTERS' SHADE

In an earlier preview, Kourtney revealed she was upset at Kim for using her wedding in Italy as a "business opportunity."

The Poosh founder confessed she was "livid" over her sister's behavior and aired her feelings on camera.

Their fight seems to have continued as Kim and Khloe shaded their older sister while on stage at a Hulu Upfront event last week.

The siblings stood before the crowd to reveal The Kardashians was renewed for 20 more episodes while also hinting at what's to come in future episodes.

Kim told the audience: "And if you guys thought last season was wild with marriages and births and the breakups, this new season is gonna be epic."

Khloe joked: "I'm so happy they gave you that line. It's a little too triggering for me that one."

She then added, "Things do get a little crazy," to which Kim responded: "Not between us, though."

Khloe quipped back, "No, you and I are good," at which point Kim appeared to take another dig, saying: "That's why we're here."

STILL FEUDING

Kim took another shot at Kourtney this week when she shared a throwback video from the family's former series, Keeping Up With the Kardashians,

The video showed one of Kourtney's confessionals, in which she talked about apologizing.

"I'm not the girl that apologizes. I hate apologizing. I just always like to think that I'm right about everything," the mom of three said on the show.

Kim added the caption: "Me be like" and tagged her sister's Instagram account, seemingly hinting that their fight is not over.
