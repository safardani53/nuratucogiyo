August 2024 · 3 minute read

Leni Klum is the daughter of supermodel Heidi Klum and her former partner, Italian businessman Flavio Briatore. Later, Leni changed her last name to her stepfather Seal’s (Samuels) and back to Klum after his and Heidi’s divorce.

Leni is an aspiring model who’s following in her mother’s footsteps. And she’s doing so in a big way, she already scored the cover of ‘Vogue Germany,’ appearing alongside her look-alike mom. She’s also appeared on runways and in Intimissimi campaigns with her mom. Whether she’s rocking lingerie or a gorgeous dress, she always manages to look incredible. She has been making a name for herself on the fashion scene.
