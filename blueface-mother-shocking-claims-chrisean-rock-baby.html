RAPPER Blueface and his mother have taken their family feud public once again after the birth of Blueface's latest child with Chrisean Rock.

Blueface, real name Johnathan Jamall Porter, has gone viral on social media multiple times due to his explosive relationships with both his mother and Chrisean, who gave birth to the couple's first child, a boy, on September 3.

In a shocking development after the birth, Blueface's mother, Karlissa Saffold, wildly suggested that Blueface and Chrisean could be related but didn't show any proof of her accusation.

In a video, Saffold said that Chrisean's mother's last name is Dorsey, supposedly the same surname of other people in Saffold's family.

"Chrisean momma is a Dorsey," said Saffold. "She a Dorsey."

Saffold didn't explain who in her family had the last name and neither she nor Blueface share that name.

Blueface has had a long history of strife with his mother, whom he said has caused irreparable damage in his life.

In 2019, Blueface infamously kicked his mother and sister out of his Los Angeles home, removing their belongings when one of his then-girlfriends reportedly refused to greet Saffold.

The rapper's sister, Kali, recorded the incident on Instagram Live, showing Blueface confronting her and their mother.

The video shows Blueface seemingly kicking at one of the women before throwing their stuff out the door.

Blueface shared surveillance footage of the confrontation and explained that his sister came to his home unannounced and argued with him as his young son was present.

"Sense [sic] they wanna go viral so bad this the real story my mom got tired of my broke a** sister free loading at her house so she brought her to mine an I wasn’t going for it," he said.

"I already got her an apartment guess that wasn’t enough she want my car she want me to buy her a car off the lot an some more stuff."

As for his mother, Blueface explained that he wasn't allowed to stay at her home in the past and was forced to live out of his car.

"I was homeless in my own car for years ain’t nobody wanna let me stay at they house I had to thug it out."

Last year, Blueface slammed his mother after she made more wild claims about his supposed treatment toward Christean.

"Y'all sent this girl back here knowing she's in harms way! I bet not here nothing about the next episode from none of y'all.

"That girl old enough to know that's not love. Bobby didn't love Whitney a.. neither. They see a couple [of] dollars and think that's love (sic)."

Blueface responded: "None my mom say (sic) is true. I don't even know that lady anymore I lost her to the internet."

Saffold hit back, saying: "You're done, you had one job and that was to open the gate for those of us who earned it. You couldn't even do that."
