His football experience and accomplishments so far

Francisco started his football career with Vianense before moving on to Porto, Braga, and Palmeiras Braga before making his professional debut with Braga B in the Second Portuguese Division in 2016 at the age of 16. He finished the season with two goals and three assists for the club and made his first team debut in 2018. In 2019, he scored his first goal for the squad, helping the team win the 2019-20 Taca da Liga. Barcelona signed him in January 2020. He scored three goals for Barcelona and helped the squad win the Copa del Rey in 2020-21.

He has six Portugal caps and has represented Portugal at various levels. He helped the UEFA European Under-19 Championship triumph in 2018 and was awarded the UEFA European Under-19 Championship best scorer.

He dedicates his achievement to his father.

Francisco adores his father, Goncalo Trincao, and holds him in high regard. When he was signed by Barcelona, he donned the number 17 to commemorate his father. He likes the number since it is his father’s birth date. He claimed to be the European Under-19 champion with the number. Both of his parents have supported his football career from the start and have relocated to Barcelona to be closer to their son.

According to Francisco, he is not training alone; his parents are also training and transporting him to and from the training. His father transported him 100 kilometers back and forth from his village of Viana to Northern Portugal for his training at Porto many days a week. His family consists of his parents and his older sister, Leonor. Leonor is two years his senior and a fashion blogger. Aside from that, there is one other member of the family: Summer, or “Verano” in Spanish.

What are his qualities?

Francisco’s mental strength is one of his many strengths. He is looking forward to playing as many games as possible in order to score more goals, assist more teammates, and help the team win championships. Scott Sellars, Wolves’ technical director, said that Trincao has been on their radar for quite some time and that he had a plethora of abilities. Being signed from Braga to Barcelona was enough to demonstrate his talent. He went on to say that Trincao has the ability to drift, move the ball quickly across two or three yards, change direction quickly, and score and create. Diogo Viana, a close friend, and former Braga colleague highlighted his one-on-one, one-on-two capacity to unleash the game with his talent as one of his strongest traits.

Net Worth

Francisco agreed to terms with Barcelona on a five-year deal for €31 million. He is on a loan period for a hefty €29.5 million. His current net worth is believed to be $20 million as of August 2023.
