May 2024 · 2 minute read

Tom Selleck – and his mustache – have been on TV for years. Take a look at his best photos right here.

Tom Selleck, born Jan 29. 1945, is a celebrated actor known for his roles in TV shows like Magnum P.I., Friends, and Blue Bloods, one of his move beloved parts. He’s played the part of NYC Police Commissioner Frank Reagan on the hit CBS series since September 2010 and the show is still on to this day.

On the big screen, Tom has appeared in movies like Quigley Down Under, Mr. Baseball, and Lassiter. Most recently, Tom made a guest appearance during the HBO Max reunion special of Friends. He played Monica Geller’s boyfriend Richard on the beloved sitcom.

Tom has been married twice: first to Jacqueline Ray from 1971 to 1982, and then to Jillie Mack, whom he’s been with since 1987. He has two kids: son Kevin, whom he adopted while married to Jacqueline, and daughter Hannah, whom he welcomed with Jillie in 1988.
