Truth or Dare thriller Nerve came out on Digital HD on Oct. 11, and whether you saw it in theaters or downloaded it now, we’re sure you cringed at the part where Emma Roberts, 25, and Dave Franco, 31, get their clothes, wallets, and shoes stolen while trying on expensive clothes at the infamous department store Bergdorf Goodman. Of course, they have no choice but to run out of the store in nothing but their undies, and it’s painfully awkward to watch. But guess what? It was way MORE awkward for the stars to film!

‘Nerve’: See Pics Of Dave Franco & Emma Roberts From The Movie

“Not the most fun scene in the world to shoot,” Dave admitted in an EXCLUSIVE behind-the-scenes clip from HollywoodLife.com. “The crew did say that they were going to get naked or at least in their underwear for a show of solidarity and I haven’t seen that, but it’s been okay.” Too funny!

“Nothing was as difficult as having to be in my bra and underwear in Bergdorf’s, I’ll tell you that,” said Emma. “We were like ‘after we run through in our underwear we want a large pizza and cupcakes’ because obviously we were trying to eat healthy before doing the bra and underwear stuff so I was like’ I want pizza immediately when we finish that.’ That was our request, that was our only diva request on set.” How can you blame her?! Being in your undies on set is awkward in general, but to do it in the middle of one of NYC’s most famous clothing stores?! Forget it!

Nerve is out on digital now and will be available o Blu-ray and DVD on October 25th. If you haven’t see it yet, watch it now!
