The Voice fans think Gwen Stefani is 'pregnant' after she reveals 'baby bump' in minidress for shows

May 2024 · 2 minute read

GWEN Stefani's fans have spotted a "baby bump" in her showstopping outfit for The Voice finale.

The singer wowed in a plunging leopard-print minidress and fishnet tights on Monday night.

Gwen, 53, showed off her incredible figure in the bold dress, which featured ruffled statement shoulders and an A-line skirt.

The Voice coach layered a gold string bikini underneath and finished off the look with black stiletto boots.

The No Doubt star pulled a series of flirty poses for the festive video, which was set to her version of Jingle Bells.

One fan claimed the mother-of-three looked like she had a baby bump in the clip.

Gwen has three children with her ex-husband Gavin Rossdale - Kingston, 16, Zuma, 14, and Apollo, eight.

She met Gavin in 1995 when No Doubt and Bush were on tour together.

They got married in 2002 before going their separate ways in 2015.

Gwen found love again with Blake Shelton, 46, after they met on The Voice in 2015.

She announced on Instagram that the couple got engaged in October 2020, and they tied the knot in July 2021 in an intimate ceremony at his Oklahoma ranch.

Reflecting on their relationship, Blake told Rolling Stone in 2016: “If you had told me that that’s who I’m gonna end up with, I would have thought you were crazy.

“But she became my closest ally."

He was previously married to country icon Miranda Lambert and Kaynette Williams.
