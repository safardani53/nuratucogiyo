June 2024 · 2 minute read

CHRISTMAS Day is just around the corner and many of us are most likely thinking about presents, Turkey and the tasty box of Heroes we’ve got just waiting to be opened.

However, shoppers have been warned to ‘brace themselves’, as Easter eggs (yes, you heard that correctly), have been spotted in supermarkets.

It's four months before the Easter weekend and with Christmas chocolate still flooding the shelves, many have been left totally stunned at the finding.

One shopper, who was shocked when she spotted Mini Eggs chocolate eggs in her local Tesco, took to social media to alert other shoppers.

Posting on social media, on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, a private Facebook group with a whopping 2.3 million members, Ria Phillips shared a picture of the Easter eggs and wrote: “Brace yourselves people ....”

She then added: “Easter is on its way ...
