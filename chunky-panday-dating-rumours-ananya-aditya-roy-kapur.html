Chunky Pandays warning as he reacts to dating rumours between daughter Ananya and Aditya Roy Kapu

May 2024 · 3 minute read

CHUNKY Panday has broken his silence on his daughter Ananya's love life saying that any man his girls date "has to be better than him".

Ananya Panday, 24, and fellow Bollywood star Aditya Roy Kapur, 37, have been making headlines for their rumoured romance.

Chunky said relationship speculations are a part and parcel of being an actor.

"Such things are bound to happen," the 60-year-old actor told Bollywood Bubble in a YouTube interview.

"They say you live by the sword you die by the sword.

"We are in the glamour profession and this is collateral damage.
