Scott Richard Weiland is an American musician, singer and songwriter. During a career spanning three decades, Weiland is best known as the lead singer of Stone Temple Pilots from 1989 to 2002 and from 2008 to 2013, with six records. He was also the lead vocalist for the supergroup Velvet Revolver from 2003-2008, recording two albums and recording an album with another supergroup, Art of Anarchy. Weiland has also established himself as a solo artist and has collaborated with several other musicians throughout his career. Weiland is known for his flamboyant and chaotic personality on stage; He is also known to change his appearance and vocal style often, because he uses a loudspeaker at concerts to create vocal effects, and for his struggles with drug addiction. . Currently regarded by many as a talented and versatile singer, Weiland has been honored by Hit Parader as one of the top 100 Heavy Metal singers. In 2012, Weiland formed the Wildabouts support group.
