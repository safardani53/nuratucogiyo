Parachute Regiment troops who filmed sex romps with woman on Army base broke NO laws, police say

October 2024 · 3 minute read

A PARA gang-bang on an Army base broke no laws police have concluded.

The troops involved could still be kicked out for shaming their crack "air-porn" unit.

Randy Toms filmed a wild group-sex romp at Merville Barracks in Colchester, Essex, home of 16 Air Assault Brigade – the UK's military quick reaction force.

The X-rated clips showed junior troops from 3rd Battalion the Parachute Regiment bonking a redheaded woman in three parts of a dormitory block.

A probe found the woman was signed on to camp 31 times in 5 months – roughly once every five days.

Red Cap military cops ruled out suggestions the tattooed woman was a sex worker.
