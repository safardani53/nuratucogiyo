DANIELLE Colby has posted new photos showing off her rarely-seen happy family.

The 48-year-old shared the pictures on her Instagram Feed which pictured her, her 22-year-old daughter Memphis, and her reclusive son Miles, 26.

She also got tattoos with her fiancé Jeremy Scheuch and two young men who appear to be Jeremy's sons.

Danielle's matching tattoos with her children depict a mama wolf and two baby wolves.

Each of her children got a baby wolf tattoo, while she got the mother tattooed with a crescent moon behind it.

With Jeremy's family, she got a ripped ticket stub from a show in Chicago.
