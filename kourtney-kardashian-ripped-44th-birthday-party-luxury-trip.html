Kourtney Kardashian ripped for over-the-top 44th birthday party with luxury trip, hundreds of flower

February 2024 · 3 minute read

KOURTNEY Kardashian has posted a slew of photos from her extravagant birthday celebrations.

The Hulu star posted on her Instagram grid and Stories, showing off her luxurious treats.

On her grid post, Kourtney revealed plenty of jaw-dropping moments from her big day.

The first image in the carousel showed a huge four-poster bed, surrounded by fresh bouquets of roses and artfully-arranged petals.

She also posted a clip of herself blowing out the candles atop a rich chocolate cake, followed with a recording of her husband Travis Barker documenting the moment.

In the next video, the newly-44-year-old captured a stunning bouquet of colorful flowers.

Behind the massive floral arrangement, an arch of metallic pink balloons read: "Happy birthday mom. Love M P R."

The initials stood for Kourtney's three children; Mason, Penelope, and Reign.

The eldest Kardashian sister then filmed the spectacular view from a rustic balcony, with sun and clear skies.

It seemed that she'd gone on a vacation away with Travis, with the setting very different from her own $9 million modern mansion.

Kourtney also snapped a pic of a healthy meal out, followed by a video of even more flowers.

Finishing her post, she shared a video of a mariachi band, who had been hired out privately to sing happy birthday to her.

FAN REACTION

Kourtney captioned her post: "Feeling so overwhelmed with gratitude for all of the birthday love and wishes. Turning 44 was a dream."

However, some of the reality TV star's followers weren't quite as impressed by the flashy events shown.

One person said: "Holy smokes. Amazing to see but those flowers alone are probably worth more than my yearly income."

A second added: "Wouldn’t it be nice if others unfortunate people could experience this."

A third advised: "Switch lives with somebody who doesn’t have the things you do for one day …. You will soon see what reality is."

One person pointed out the impressive floral arrangements, and said: "Exactly! I thought of the cost of them & how much good that money would do for underprivileged children!"

Another snarked: "Obscene display of wealth, per the usual. Haven’t heard of any philanthropic endeavors on your part, ever."

PARTY DOESN'T STOP

Despite the negative reaction from some fans, Kourtney posted even more moments of her birthday on her Instagram Stories.

One sweet image was reposted from Travis, who captured his wife as she sat on their rose petal-decorated bed and opened a gift.

Another showed a further two cakes that she'd received.

One classic vanilla cake was topped with fruit, and read "Happy Birthday, Kourtney" in the icing.
