Bronson Pinchot’s Bio: Upbringing and Career

Born on May 20, 1959, to Henry Pinchot and Rosina Pinchot, Bronson was raised in Southern California alongside his two brothers and a sister. His brother, Justin Pinchot is an actor as well. Mother, Rosina was an Italian American while his father was of Russian descent.

Bronson graduated from South Pasadena High School excelling in French, theater, and choir. He got a full scholarship to Yale University where he began his Yale career in fine art. A casting director noticed him during his play in college, which led to his film debut, Risky Business.

Appearing in several feature films, Bronson quickly made a big name for himself from long-running ABC sitcom, Perfect Strangers. In 2009 and 2014, he narrated over 100 audiobooks that won him a number of awards. Audible.com also chose him as their Narrator of the Year in 2010.
