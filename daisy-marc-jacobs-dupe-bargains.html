Home Bargains 'new perfume dupes are out now' including4.99 replica of Marc Jacobs'Daisy that'

February 2024 · 3 minute read

BARGAIN-hunting shoppers are scrambling to Home Bargains after the store launched a new range of perfumes that even boasts a dupe of Marc Jacobs' Daisy for a fiver.

Whether it's something musky or a bit more floral, a bottle of perfume is sure to be on the wishlist for many beauty lovers this Christmas.

But if you're yet to dash to the stores to splurge out on a posh designer scent for your loved ones, make sure to check out the latest fragrance range at Home Bargains.

According to money-saving shoppers, the retailer has come out with on a number of affordable dupes of designer scents, including a £4.99 replica of the summery Daisy by Marc Jacobs.

One customer, @dontmesswiththedupes, took to TikTok to inform fellow bargain lovers about the cheap deals - and looks like we're spoilt for choice.

As well as getting your hands on a copy of Daisy that's a whopping 20 times cheaper, Home Bargains fans can now also bag Firetrap Heavenly.

This £4.99 scent, the video showed, is thought to be a close match of the sweet and refreshing aroma of Angel by Mugler, £107.

A little maths and you can calculate you're saving more than a whopping £100.

Meanwhile, if you're on the lookout for One Million but don't fancy spending an eye-watering £92 then we suggest you check out One Billion at Home Bargains.

This little number, which also comes as a body spray, sells for under a fiver, making it the perfect last-minute Christmas pressie.

Other bargain buys now landing on shelves include a budget-friendly alternative to the cult favourite Boss by Hugo Boss called Firetrap Homme.

Unlike the real deal which will set you back by £60 in stores such as Boots, the more affordable fragrance at the discount store scans for £4.99 at the tills.

If someone you know is after Polo Red by Ralph Lauren that's going for almost £57 on the high street, then the dupe whizz advised to first see if you like Jour Pour Homme.

Although the bottles look slightly different, the bargain buy from Home Bargains also comes in the form of a body wash - a win-win on a budget.

Other must-have beauty essentials in the range include a replica of Dior's Sauvage called Firetrap Nocturnal, as well as a dupe of Y by Yves Saint Laurent called Blue Water.

Priced just £4.99 and £3.99, these bargain perfumes will save you a fortune - which all of us could do with during the festive madness.

If you're a fan of cheap perfumes, then @dontmesswiththedupes is your new best friend.

Regularly sharing the latest bargains to hit the high-street, the beauty lover has amassed close to 54k followers on TikTok.

Some of their latest finds include a Tom Ford replica that's a whopping 40 times cheaper, a Dyson dupe at Aldi, as well as an affordable perfume range at Superdrug.
