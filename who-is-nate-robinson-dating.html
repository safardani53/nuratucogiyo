Nathaniel Cornelius Robinson is an American professional basketball player who last played for Guaros de Lara of the Liga Profesional de Baloncesto (LPB). Born in Seattle, Robinson played college basketball for the University of Washington in Seattle and was the 21st pick in the 2005 NBA draft. The 5’9″ point guard has also played for the New York Knicks, Boston Celtics, Oklahoma City Thunder, Golden State Warriors, Chicago Bulls, and Denver Nuggets. Robinson is the NBA’s first three-time slam dunk champion.

He was named AAA State Player of the Year while playing high school basketball at Rainier Beach High School in Washington.
