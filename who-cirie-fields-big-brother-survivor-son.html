Who is Cirie Fields? | The US Sun

February 2024 · 3 minute read

CIRIE Fields tested her physical skills on four seasons of the CBS reality competition series Survivor.

Often regarded as one of the best Survivor players to never win, fans want to know what she's up to now.

Who is Cirie Fields?

Cirie Fields is a nurse and television personality who was born on July 18, 1970, in Jersey City, New Jersey.

She first rose to fame in 2006 after she debuted on Survivor: Panama, where she finished in fourth place.

Two years later, she returned to the Survivor franchise, placing third on Survivor: Micronesia.

Cirie then went on to compete on Survivor: Heroes vs. Villains, and Survivor: Game Changers, but she was ultimately never able to secure a win.

While she might've lost all four seasons of Survivor, she emerged victorious on Season 1 of the Peacock show, The Traitors, after defeating Andie Vanacore and Quentin Jiles.

"I was in shock," Cirie recalled during an interview with E! News.

"One, I won finally, and two, the other two people there with me were so hurt and upset. So, I really couldn't enjoy the win while watching them be devastated.

"I got that feeling later on after all the emotions kind of dwindled and I got to speak to them and I knew that everybody was okay, then I really got to go, 'Oh s***,' I just won,'" she continued.

"My inbox stays full... It's almost shocking to get that much love."

Cirie also secured a group win alongside Big Brother and The Amazing Race alums Janelle Pierzina and Rachel Reilly on Peacock's Snake in the Grass.

What is Cirie Fields doing now?

Cirie has come a long way from her Survivor days but she hasn't shied away from her passion to compete.

After competing on Survivor, Snake in the Grass, and The Traitors, she is now trying her hand in the Big Brother house.

Typically, Big Brother has 16 houseguests, but in a shocking twist, the show added a 17th, which just so happened to be Cirie.

What's even more shocking is that she's the mother of fellow houseguest Jared Fields.

Cirie made her Season 25 Big Brother debut at the end of the season's premiere.

“Surprise, guess who’s back? It is me, Cirie, from Survivor seasons 12, 16, 20 and 34,” Cirie said in a clip that was posted on Big Brother's Twitter page.

“If you don’t know me, you’re about to get to know me on Big Brother.”

Cirie will be competing against her son, America Lopez, Blue Kim, Bowie Jane, Cameron Hardin, Cory Wurtenberger, Felicia Cannon, Hisam Goueli, Izzy Gleicher, Jag Bains, Kirsten Elwin, Luke Balentine, Matt Klotz, Mecole Hayes, Red Utley, and Reilly Smedley.

Who is Cirie Fields’ son, Jared?

Jared Fields is a reality television personality from Norwalk, Connecticut.

Outside of being known as Cirie's son, he is known for his career as an exterminator.

Prior to the start of the season, Jared sat down with Parade, revealing that he wanted to keep his mother's identity a secret.

“Imagine you hearing on a basketball court that you’re playing against LeBron’s son, you would think that he has the most game ever, and you want to take that chance.  I’m not taking a chance,” he told the outlet.
